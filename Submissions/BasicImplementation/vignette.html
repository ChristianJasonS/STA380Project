Vignette

This vignette serves as a showcase of how the functions written in inverse_transformation.R can be used for simulating the Kumaraswamy distribution and arcsine distribution.

Kumaraswamy distribution

set.seed(1)
n <- 10000
a <- 0.7
b <- 0.3
xs <- is_kumaraswamy(n, a, b)
draw_histogram(xs, dist="kumaraswamy", a=a, b=b)


draw_index_scatter(xs)


draw_table(xs, "kumaraswamy", a, b)
Simulated Theoretical
Mean 0.7170607 0.7187660
Variance 0.0996450 0.0987477 

draw_index_scatter(xs)

Arcsine distribution

set.seed(1)
